Who We Are

ABOUT LE REVE

“Le Reve” is a French word meaning “The Dream”. We chose this because, a home is a dream for every one. In the same way our dream is to serve every individual to turn out their dream to come true.

Le Reve is an establishment developed by two engineers having more than 15 years experience in high rise buildings both commercial and residential. Le Reve serves in the field of architectural designing and project management consultancy for more than five years. The experience achieved by these two engineers leads Le Reve to one of the top most consultancies in Kottayam within these years.

At Le Reve, we prioritize customized solutions for every project, understanding that each one is unique. Our team includes architects and interior designers, and civil engineers who work collaboratively to cover all aspects of the project, from design to execution. We aim to provide innovative and sustainable solutions that benefit our clients in the long-term, keeping up-to-date with industry trends and technologies. Communication is key, and we maintain an open line of communication with our clients throughout the entire process. Our commitment to customization, innovation, and communication has helped us establish a reputation for delivering exceptional services that exceed our clients' expectations.
